# 🎯 Reaction Time Game

A simple and interactive **Reaction Time Game** built using **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**. The game measures how quickly you react when the screen changes from red to green.

## 📌 Features

- 🎮 Click anywhere or press the **Space** key to play
- ⏳ Random waiting time before the signal appears
- ⚡ Measures your reaction time in milliseconds
- 🏆 Keeps track of your best score
- 📊 Displays your most recent score
- 🚨 Detects early clicks and asks you to try again
- 📱 Simple and responsive interface

---

## 🖼️ Preview

When the game starts:

- **Blue Screen:** Ready to start
- **Red Screen:** Wait...
- **Green Screen:** Click as fast as possible!
- **Result Screen:** Displays your reaction time and best score.

## 🎮 How to Play

1. Click anywhere on the screen or press the **Space** key.
2. The screen will turn **red**.
3. Wait until it turns **green**.
4. As soon as it turns green, click immediately or press **Space**.
5. Your reaction time will be displayed.
6. Try to beat your best score!

⚠️ Clicking before the green screen appears will result in a **Too Early!** message.
